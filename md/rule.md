# Mesos - Comprehensive Rule Reference

This document provides a detailed breakdown of the game mechanics, setup, and scoring for **Mesos**, based on the official rulebook.

---

## 1. Game Components
*   **2 Turn Order Tiles**: Dual-sided, used based on player count.
*   **7 Offer Tiles**: Form the Offer Track (A through G).
*   **94 Tribe Cards**: 84 Characters, 10 Events (divided into Era I, II, and III).
*   **2 Final Event Cards**: Placed at the very bottom of the Tribe deck.
*   **21 Building Cards**: 6 Era I, 7 Era II, 8 Era III.
*   **48 Food Tokens**: Denominations of 1 and 5.
*   **75 Prestige Point (PP) Tokens**: Used for scoring (positive and negative).
*   **5 Totems**: One per player.
*   **5 Summary Cards**: Reference for players.

---

## 2. Setup

1.  **Offer Track**: Filter Offer Tiles based on player count (3+, 4+, 5 tags). Arrange them alphabetically (A-G).
2.  **Turn Order Tile**: Use the side matching the player count. Place it at the top-left of the Offer Track.
3.  **Tribe Deck**:
    *   Filter cards by player count.
    *   Separate into Era I, II, and III. Shuffle each.
    *   Stack: 2 Final Events (bottom) -> Era III -> Era II -> Era I (top).
4.  **Initial Cards**:
    *   **Lower Row**: Draw (Players + 1) cards. If an Event is drawn, place it in the Upper Row and draw another for the Lower Row until full.
    *   **Upper Row**: Draw (Players + 4) cards.
5.  **Buildings**:
    *   Separate by Era. Shuffle each.
    *   Draw random buildings per Era based on player count:
        *   2 players: Era I (1), Era II (2), Era III (3)
        *   3 players: Era I (2), Era II (2), Era III (4)
        *   4 players: Era I (2), Era II (3), Era III (4)
        *   5 players: Era I (2), Era II (3), Era III (5)
    *   Place **Era I** buildings face-up in the Upper Row (right of Tribe cards). Keep Era II and III face-down nearby.
6.  **Starting Food**:
    *   1st Player: 2 Food
    *   2nd & 3rd Player: 3 Food
    *   4th & 5th Player: 4 Food
7.  **Initial Turn Order**: Place totems randomly on the Turn Order tile.

---

## 3. Gameplay Structure
A game lasts **10 rounds** (or until the Tribe deck is empty). Each round has 2 phases:

### Phase 1: Placing Totems
In the current turn order (from top to bottom on the Turn Order tile), players move their totem to an **empty** tile on the Offer Track.

### Phase 2: Resolving Actions
Resolved from **left to right** (Tile A to G) based on totem positions:
1.  **Perform Action**:
    *   **Tile A**: (5 players only) Take 3 Food.
    *   **Tiles B-G**: Take a specific number of cards from the Upper and/or Lower rows as indicated on the tile.
    *   *Rules for taking cards*:
        *   You **cannot** take Event cards.
        *   If multiple cards are allowed, choose the order freely.
        *   If a row is empty, you get nothing from that row.
        *   **Buildings** cost Food (top-left of card). **Characters** are free.
2.  **Move to New Turn Order**: After the action, place the totem on the first available (topmost) slot of the Turn Order tile.
    *   **Food Bonus**: If the slot has a food icon (e.g., +3, +1), take that amount.
    *   **Last Slot Penalty**: The player in the last slot must **pay 1 Food**. If they have no food, they **lose 2 PP**.

---

## 4. Character Types
Characters are added to the player's tribe in columns by type:
*   **Hunters (Cacciatori)**:
    *   If the card has a "food icon" (+🥩), take 1 Food for every Hunter in your tribe immediately.
    *   Critical for the **Hunting Event**.
*   **Shamans (Sciamani)**:
    *   Display 1-3 Star symbols.
    *   Used for majority/minority comparison in the **Shamanic Ritual Event**.
*   **Artists (Artisti)**:
    *   Used for the **Cave Painting Event**.
    *   End Game: 10 PP for every 2 Artists.
*   **Inventors (Inventori)**:
    *   End Game: PP = (Number of Inventors) × (Number of *unique* invention icons).
*   **Builders (Costruttori)**:
    *   Reduce the Food cost of all future Buildings by the amount shown (top-right of card). Minimum cost is always 0.
    *   End Game: Grant PP shown (bottom-left).
*   **Gatherers (Raccoglitori)**:
    *   Provide a 3 Food discount during the **Sustenance Event**. (They do not give food directly).

---

## 5. Event Resolution
Events in the **Lower Row** are resolved at the end of the round. If multiple events exist, **Sustenance** is always resolved last.

1.  **Sustenance (Sostentamento)**:
    *   Pay 1 Food per Character card in your tribe. (Buildings don't count).
    *   Gatherers provide a -3 Food discount total.
    *   **Penalty**: For every character you cannot feed, lose the PP indicated on the Event card (increases by Era). You keep the unfed characters.
    *   *Note*: You cannot choose to lose PP to save food.
2.  **Hunting (Caccia)**:
    *   Take 1 Food and the indicated PP (increases by Era) for every Hunter in your tribe.
3.  **Shamanic Ritual (Rituale Sciamanico)**:
    *   Most Stars: Gain indicated PP.
    *   Fewest Stars: Lose indicated PP.
    *   Ties: All tied players gain/lose the points.
4.  **Cave Paintings (Pitture Rupestri)**:
    *   Low Artist Count (top row): Lose indicated PP.
    *   High Artist Count (bottom row): Gain indicated PP per Artist.

---

## 6. End of Round Procedure
1.  **Resolve Events** in the Lower Row.
2.  **Discard**: Remove all Tribe and Event cards from the Lower Row. **Buildings stay**.
3.  **Shift**: Move all Tribe and Event cards from the Upper Row to the Lower Row. **Buildings stay**.
4.  **Refill**: Draw (Players + 4) cards to refill the Upper Row.
    *   If a card from a **new Era** is revealed, the **Era Transition** occurs immediately.

---

## 7. Era Transition
When a card of the next Era (II or III) is revealed during refill:
1.  **Era III Start Only**: Discard any buildings in the Lower Row.
2.  **Era II & III Start**: Move current Upper Row buildings to the Lower Row (right of Tribe cards).
3.  **Refill Buildings**: Place the new Era's buildings face-up in the Upper Row.
4.  Continue the round normally.

---

## 8. Final Scoring
Triggered after 10 rounds or when the Tribe deck is empty. Resolve all visible events (including Upper Row), with Sustenance last.

**Total PP = (PP gained during game) + (End-game bonuses):**
*   **Builders**: Sum of PP on cards.
*   **Inventors**: (Inventors) × (Unique icons).
*   **Artists**: 10 PP per pair.
*   **Buildings**: Printed PP + PP from special effects.
*   **Tie-breaker**: Most remaining Food.

---

## 9. Specialized Building Effects (Appendix)
*   **Set Bonus**: 5 Food when completing a set of 6 different character types.
*   **Specific Discount**: -1 Food during Sustenance per character of a specific type (Artist/Inventor/Gatherer).
*   **Shaman Protection**: Do not lose PP if you have the fewest stars.
*   **Turn Order Bonus**: +1 Food when landing on a bonus space on the Turn Order tile.
*   **Inventor Bonus**: 3 Food when gaining a pair of identical invention icons.
*   **Shaman Boost**: Count as having 3 additional stars.
*   **Ritual Mastery**: Double PP if you have the most stars.
*   **Hunter Boost**: During Hunting, +1 Food and +1 PP per Hunter.
*   **Builder Mastery**: Double PP from all Builder cards.
*   **Artist Profit**: 1 Food per Artist during Cave Painting event.
*   **Set PP**: 6 PP per set of 6 different character types.
*   **Class PP**: PP for every card of a specific type.
*   **Extra Action**: After Phase 2, take 1 extra card (paying cost) from the Upper Row.
*   **Flat PP**: Instant 25 PP.
